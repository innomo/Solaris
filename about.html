About Solaris Energy

Founded in Johannesburg in 2012, we are one of South Africa's most trusted solar providers.

Our Story

Solaris Energy was started by a team of electrical engineers who believed that every South African deserved access to clean, affordable electricity. Over the past decade, we have grown from a small local team into a company with over 3,800 completed installations across the country.

We are SAPVIA and NERSA certified, and every one of our technicians is trained and accredited. Our work does not stop at installation — we are a long-term energy partner for every customer we serve.

Our Values

Integrity

Transparent pricing and honest advice — no hidden fees, ever.

Quality

We only use tier-1 certified equipment backed by manufacturer warranties.

Community

Locally rooted, we employ and train South Africans across all our regions.

Sustainability

Every watt we generate is a step toward a cleaner planet for future generations.
